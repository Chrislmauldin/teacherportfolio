Helping others comes naturally to me and this is the main reason why I wanted to serve in the Peace Corps. At Binghamton University, my passion for helping others blossomed when I joined JUMP Nations (Juvenile Urban Multicultural Program), an organization whose goal is to inspire at-risk youths in New York City.

JUMP focuses on at-risk eighth grade students, fulfilling its mission to decrease the high school dropout rate among these inner city youths and to increase their enrollment in a higher institution of learning. My participation with JUMP led to me being elected as an Executive Board. I am not only giving back to the youth but also growing from within to be a more positive role model for these students. My experience over the past four years of working with JUMP confirmed my passion for helping others and joining the Peace Corps would allow me to continue to uplift youths and others in the future.

In addition to fulfilling my passion, the Peace Corps will help prepare me for a career in international affairs, where I would help to develop economic policies abroad in order to stimulate and stabilize foreign economies. In the future I also hope to work with non-profit organizations that focus on community and youth development within developing countries, which will allow me to help people on a much larger scale. While doing this I hope to learn more about other cultures and embrace their customs.

Methods/Sessions

Typically, I don't have a set method for every individual or group that I instruct. I tend to use a different template for private courses, small groups and bigger groups. I use these templates to find where the strengths and weaknesses lie in each candidate then I structure my lessons accordingly. In terms of the sessions, they are structured as such:

Novice Learners

  • Alphabet
  • Basic Vocabulary
  • Basic Phrases

Intermediate Learners

  • More Vocabulary
  • Building Sentences
  • Basic Grammar

Advanced Learners

  • Reading & Writing Paragraphs - Short stories
  • Becoming Conversational
  • Test Skills in the field
